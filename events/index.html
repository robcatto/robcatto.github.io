Beginner Junior Race
Perisher Saturday 27 June

Paddy Palin Junior

An amazing opportunity for kids to participate in their first XC ski event at Perisher. Kids as young as 2 and as old as 16 years can participate freestyle (any style) over distances ranging from 0.5km to 2.5km. Festivities continue after a free hot dog lunch with the on snow and ski jelly snake hunt! Lots of spot prizes, yummy food and most importantly fun for all! Entries and lunch are free on the day.

Come and Try XC Skiing Open Day 1

Ever wanted to try Cross Country Skiing or learn new techniques and skills? Well here is a great opportunity to give it a go. Come along and learn the techniques used for cross country skiing by skilled professionals with a two hour lesson followed by a BBQ lunch, and a tour of the trails in the afternoon. Participants will need to bring their own cross country equipment.

Beginner Junior Race
Perisher Sunday 12 July

ACT Schools Cross Country Championships

This event is for ACT school students to race each other as individuals and/or part of a team of 3. The event is run similar to Interschools where students compete in their Interschools division (year at school) and the top 3 teams and individuals are recognised. The discipline is cross country freestyle with a mass start. The distance will be approx 1 - 1.5km. This event is part of the ACT Schools Cup.

Beginner Social & Race
Perisher Saturday 1 August

Perisher XC Ski Week

Do you want plenty of skiing, fresh air and fun for a whole week? Try Perisher XC Ski Week. Not just a series of individual events but now one big event. XC Ski Week is a week that caters for all levels of skiers whether you take part in the events or be a part of the cheering crowd. If you feel like racing, it is purely up to yourself as to how fast or slow you want to go. All participants are cheered on and skiers new to each event are especially welcome. Events include racing, Mystery Coffee Tours, try Laser Biathlon, Fast & Female (girls only!), demonstrations and waxing clinics, plus all the free skiing that you can handle.

Snowy Mountain Classic

The Snowy Mountains Classic is run where possible off the main trails and takes in beautiful natural terrain in some of the most scenic parts of the snowy mountains.
